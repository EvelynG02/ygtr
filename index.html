About Us

Welcome to York Gardens & Tearoom! We are the royal tearoom located at 1126 George Washington Memorial Highway, the Historical Corridor through York County in the beautiful state of Virginia.

As a truly authentic English tearoom, we work very hard to provide each of our guests with the most enjoyable afternoon tea service and fine dining experience. Within a short time, we are created a name for ourselves by serving our guests as though they were enjoying afternoon tea, lunch or dinner at Buckingham Palace.

Every quest is treated like royalty and our menu is no exception. At York Gardens & Tearoom, our experienced chefs are passionate about creating the very finest in delectable sweets, tea sandwiches, fresh baked scones and of course our full 4 course dinner service is not one to overlook.

We serve a variety of wonderful dishes. All our food is freshly prepared each day and we use only the finest and freshest ingredients to create our range of amazing dishes. Our food is a combination of innovation and deliciousness and our luxurious range of over 30 organic and fair trade certified teas are a must try!

Our elegant English ambiance along with our friendly and courteous staff is sure to make your afternoon tea and dining experience royal, rememberable and enjoyable.

At York Gardens & Tearoom, our focus is on making our guests happy through our inspired dishes. From our light lunch, afternoon tea service and on through our dinner service, we never make compromises and we strive continually to provide a true royal Buckingham Palace experience for our quest to fully enjoy.
